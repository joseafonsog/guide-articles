## Gamification

<strong>Gamification</strong> is te use of game style designs in non-game context to mantain the user engaged with the activities they are making.

#### This practice can have many applications:
1) Marketing
2) Inspiration
3) Health
4) Work
5) Crowdsourcing
6) Education
7) Politics
8) Tecnology design
9) Authentication
